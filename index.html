About

I grew up in a small town in central Florida. Love of learning brought me to boarding school in Massachusetts at age 12, where I read a lot and spent sunny days soaking in the waters of Walden and just generally getting into mischief. I then came to Chicago and danced and sang my way through college, receiving a BFA in Musical Theatre from CCPA at Roosevelt University. For the past 7 months, I have been attending Starter School and learning to make things for the internet that change the way we live our lives. My other hobbies include whiskey, Star Wars, painting, and writing.

Currently

I am currently attending Starter School until June 20th. I am working on an app to help theatre companies with their casting process.
